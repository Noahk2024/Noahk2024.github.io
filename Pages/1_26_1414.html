About This Blog

We have arrived in Hormuz. This is the farthest spot on our journey and the western end of the Indian Ocean. The street performances here are incredible. A man was using a monkey to show how good the tracking skills of the monkey was. While blindfolded, he could tell where the person who hit him on the head was, even when that person was hidden in the crowd. We will be heading back to China after this, stopping by old ports to collect some goods stored there for the time being.
