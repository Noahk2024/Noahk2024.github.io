About This Blog

The fleet has stopped in Kalikut. This kingdom does not seem to be a minor power, and our leaders are currently attempting to negotiate fair trade treaties with them. It looks like we will be at this port for quite a while. This is abnormal. Ships tend to come to this port, unload their goods, and trade whatever they can at the market price at the time. The king will then send officials to collect duty taxes on these trades. Large-scale price fixing is almost unheard of. This might drag on a while. I might have time to travel elsewhere on my own. The pepper traded here is of the finest quality.
