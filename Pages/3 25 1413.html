We’ve arrived at the kingdom of Champa. It is always warm here, and snow never falls. The trees and plants surrounding us are always green. This nation’s climate is almost perpetually in the 4th or 5th month of the year. Many of the aristocracy here wear elaborate headgear, and the king is a devout Buddhist. As we reach higher ranks in court, the headgear changes. Perhaps they indicate the man’s rank within the court? The doorways of all buildings never seem to exceed a certain height. They are able to produce the finest incense, which is being sold for its weight in silver. They also highly value the porcelain that we brought. Perhaps the next time we come here, the fleet should stock up on this commodity. The kingdom has many of the same animals we have back home in China. Chickens, horses, and many other animals are here. Their punishments are quite harsh however, and many involve physical harm.
