About This Blog

Traveling through Sumatra, the culture here is very similar to that of Java. There are still many street performers with their scroll-paintings, and many more Chinese merchant communities. This makes sense, the Strait of Malacca is quite the trade destination, so many communities would rise around it. Many of these Chinese merchants married into local families and have new lives here, some with close ties to the imperial court.
