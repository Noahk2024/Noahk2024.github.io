About This Blog

I have arrived at Mecca. There are all different kinds of fruits here. Grapes taste amazing. Everyone is dressed like an Arab. Cloth covers their heads. It is incredibly peaceful here. The architecture is awe inspiring. It's truly breathtaking. Everyone is devout, many people are pilgrims here just like me. The laws and rulers here govern well, and I did not see any crime within the city itself. I must now depart back to the fleet. I hope that I timed this journey well.
